About Me

I'm Wendy and welcome to my page. When I'm not crunching numbers or sitting in traffic, I'm searching for the best pizza joint. (So far it's Bar del Corso in Beacon Hill - they also have delicious arancini.) In my quiet moments I often find myself complaining about the weather and wondering why I don't live on a beach.

After my annoyance with the weather subsides I can usually be found walking around the city, exploring unknown streets and trying to find every entrance into the Seattle underground.
